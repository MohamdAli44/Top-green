Honey

Clover Honey

Clover honey is one of the types of honey that is extracted from clover flowers, from May to mid-June, and this type is known for its light yellow color

cotton flower honey

Cotton honey is the sweetest type of honey in taste. It has a distinctive and beautiful smell and its color is light yellow (amber) extracted from the nectar of cotton flower flowers. It is very sweet and is the best alternative to white sugar.

Sidr honey

Sidr honey, which is one of the best and most famous types of honey, is extracted from the nectar of Sidr trees. Sidr honey is known as a monochromatic honey.

nigella honey

Nigella sativa honey is extracted from the nectar of nigella and is not mixed with it as some believe, as this type is known for its clear smell and flavor, in addition to its dark color.

citrus honey

Citrus honey is honey extracted from citrus trees from the beginning of March until the end of April, so some call it citrus honey, and it is extracted from several citrus trees such as: orange, grapefruit, mandelina, lemon, tangerine, and bitter orange.

royal jelly

Royal jelly is one of the best types of honey that contains very high nutrients. It is a milky secretion produced by worker bees in the hive.
